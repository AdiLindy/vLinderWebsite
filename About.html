ABOUT

Engineering Portfolio

I’m a High school student with an interest in engineering. For the past years, I’ve been teaching myself as much as I can about the engineering process through various projects. I enjoy being involved in every step of the process by being a generalist. As of now, I feel what I do is largely hobbyist and unimportant and that was fine for a while to challenge myself and learn. But recently I have wanted to devote what I’ve learned to something more impactful. With every project I work on comes a very gratifying feeling, but it could never be as rewarding as making something that could help others. I call the compilation of what I make vLinder and embellish my projects with a butterfly icon. Vlinder means butterfly in Dutch. When I was younger I had an odd fear of butterflies and most bugs, but it began to cross a line where I had an interest in them. And that’s something I like to explore when fear becomes a fascination.
